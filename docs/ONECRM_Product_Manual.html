1. Introduction

ONECRM is a multi-tenant customer relationship management platform built for education and study-abroad organisations. It unifies lead generation, student application pipelines, visa tracking, agency partner management, HR operations, and self-service portals in one system.

ONECRM sits in the ApplyUniNow ecosystem and is delivered by AUN Tech Consulting.

1.1 What you can do with ONECRM

  • Capture, score, and convert marketing leads
  • Manage student profiles, university applications, documents, offers, fees, and visas
  • Onboard agency partners, track referrals, and calculate commissions
  • Run HR processes: employees, recruitment, attendance, leave, performance, and payroll
  • Control users, roles, and module access per tenant
  • Give students and agents dedicated self-service experiences

2.2 First login checklist

  1. Open the correct login page for your role.
  2. Enter your email and password. Use Forgot password if needed.
  3. Change your password when prompted (first-time or reset flows).
  4. Confirm the modules visible in the left sidebar match your responsibilities.
  5. Open Notifications (/notifications) to review unread alerts.
Tip: If a menu item is missing, your role may lack permission or the module may be disabled for your tenant. Contact your Global Admin.

3.2 Permission model (summary)

  • Module permissions such as VIEW_MARKETING, MANAGE_STUDENT_CRM, MANAGE_AGENCY_CRM.

  • Tenant module switches decide whether Marketing, Student CRM, Agency CRM, HR, and Admin appear at all.

4. Marketing Module

Path: /marketing

Use Marketing to generate demand, nurture prospects, and hand qualified leads to Student CRM.

4.1 Lead Management

Path: /marketing/lead-management

  • Create and update leads with contact details, source, and preferred programme information.
  • Track status through the pipeline (for example New → Working → Converted / Lost).
  • Rate interest (Hot / Warm / Cold) and assign counsellors.
  • Log activities: notes, calls, email, SMS, WhatsApp, and meetings.
  • Promote qualified leads into Student CRM when ready to start applications.

4.2 Campaigns

Path: /marketing/campaigns

  • Plan and launch campaigns across email, SMS, WhatsApp, social, and PPC channels.
  • Track campaign messaging and performance alongside lead intake.

4.3 Marketing Analytics

Path: /marketing/marketing-analytics

  • Review channel performance, funnel metrics, and intake trends.
  • Monitor student and agency funnels to spot conversion bottlenecks.

5. Student CRM

Path: /student-crm

Student CRM is the operational core for counselling teams managing applicants from enquiry through enrolment.

5.1 Student Management

Path: /student-crm/student-management

  • Maintain student profiles, academics, and contact data.
  • Capture study plans (country, university, course, intake).
  • Link preferred universities/courses from the CRM catalogue.
  • Manage checklists and supporting documents across process stages.

5.2 Applications

Path: /student-crm/applications

Track each university application through stages such as:

  1. Draft
  2. Documents pending
  3. Submitted
  4. Under review
  5. Offer received
  6. Accepted / Rejected
  7. Visa process
  8. Enrolled

Staff can upload documents, record offers, assign tasks, advance stages, and manage fees/payments.

5.3 Visa management

Path: /student-crm/visa-management

  • Track visa status and checklist documents per student/application.
  • Coordinate pre-CAS / I-20 and related milestones as applicable by destination.

5.4 CRM Settings

Path: /student-crm/settings

  • Maintain catalogue data: countries, universities, courses, and industries.
  • Keep form options aligned with ApplyUniNow university/course mappings.

5.5 Process checklist stages

Applications may also follow operational process stages, for example:

  • Gathering checklist → University application → Financial evidence
  • After I-20 / Pre-CAS → Visa → Pre-departure → On arrival → Pre-requisite

6.2 Partner onboarding stages

  1. Registered
  2. Documents submitted
  3. Agreement signed
  4. Verified
  5. Approved
  6. Active

6.3 Agent workflow

  1. Register via /agent-login or /register (approval may be required).
  2. Complete onboarding: upload KYC/business documents and sign the agreement.
  3. Share your referral code / co-branded links to capture leads.
  4. Track referred students and application progress under My Students.
  5. Review commissions and statements under My Commissions.
Agents only see students and applications attributed to their partnership. Commission creation and status changes are admin-controlled unless explicitly granted.

12. Common Workflows

12.1 Lead → Student → Application

  1. Create or capture a lead in Marketing → Lead Management.
  2. Qualify the lead with activities and counsellor assignment.
  3. Promote the lead to Student CRM.
  4. Create study plans and university applications.
  5. Collect documents, submit, track offers, then advance to visa and enrolment.

12.2 Agency referral → commission

  1. Agent completes onboarding and receives an active partner profile.
  2. Lead or student is attributed via referral code / agency reference.
  3. Application progresses; commissions may auto-generate on configured triggers (for example enrolled or visa approved).
  4. Admin verifies commissions and processes payouts; agent reviews statement.

12.3 Student self-service payment

  1. Student signs in to the applicant portal.
  2. Opens Payments for outstanding fees.
  3. Completes checkout; staff can verify payment status in Student CRM.
